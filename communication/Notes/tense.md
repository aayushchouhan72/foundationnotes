# English Tenses – Complete Notes (with Hindi Reference)

There are **12 tenses** in English.

## 1. Present Tenses (वर्तमान काल)

### A. Simple Present Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + V1 + Object
- He/She/It + V1+s/es

**Hindi Clue:**

- ता हूँ, ती हूँ, ते हैं

**Examples:**

- मैं स्कूल जाता हूँ। → I go to school.
- वह क्रिकेट खेलता है। → He plays cricket.

**Uses:**

- Daily habits
- Facts
- Routine actions

---

### B. Present Continuous Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + am/is/are + V1+ing

**Hindi Clue:**

- रहा हूँ, रही हूँ, रहे हैं

**Examples:**

- मैं खाना खा रहा हूँ। → I am eating food.
- वे खेल रहे हैं। → They are playing.

**Uses:**

- Action happening right now

---

### C. Present Perfect Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + has/have + V3

**Hindi Clue:**

- चुका है, लिया है, दिया है, गया है

**Examples:**

- मैंने खाना खा लिया है। → I have eaten food.
- वह घर जा चुका है। → He has gone home.

**Uses:**

- Action completed recently

---

### D. Present Perfect Continuous Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + has/have been + V1+ing + since/for

**Hindi Clue:**

- से ... रहा हूँ

**Examples:**

- मैं दो घंटे से पढ़ रहा हूँ। → I have been studying for two hours.
- वह सुबह से काम कर रहा है। → He has been working since morning.

**Uses:**

- Action started in the past and still continues

---

# 2. Past Tenses (भूतकाल)

### A. Simple Past Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + V2

**Hindi Clue:**

- ता था, ती थी, ते थे, या, ई, ए

**Examples:**

- मैं स्कूल गया। → I went to school.
- उसने खाना खाया। → He ate food.

**Uses:**

- Completed action in the past

---

### B. Past Continuous Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + was/were + V1+ing

**Hindi Clue:**

- रहा था, रही थी, रहे थे

**Examples:**

- मैं पढ़ रहा था। → I was studying.
- वे खेल रहे थे। → They were playing.

**Uses:**

- Action going on in the past

---

### C. Past Perfect Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + had + V3

**Hindi Clue:**

- चुका था, लिया था, दिया था

**Examples:**

- मैं खाना खा चुका था। → I had eaten food.
- वह घर जा चुका था। → He had gone home.

**Uses:**

- One past action completed before another

---

### D. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + had been + V1+ing + since/for

**Hindi Clue:**

- से ... रहा था

**Examples:**

- मैं दो घंटे से पढ़ रहा था। → I had been studying for two hours.
- वह सुबह से काम कर रहा था। → He had been working since morning.

**Uses:**

- Continuous action before a point in the past

---

# 3. Future Tenses (भविष्य काल)

### A. Simple Future Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + will + V1

**Hindi Clue:**

- गा, गी, गे

**Examples:**

- मैं स्कूल जाऊँगा। → I will go to school.
- वह क्रिकेट खेलेगा। → He will play cricket.

**Uses:**

- Future action

---

### B. Future Continuous Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + will be + V1+ing

**Hindi Clue:**

- रहा होगा, रही होगी, रहे होंगे

**Examples:**

- मैं पढ़ रहा होऊँगा। → I will be studying.
- वे खेल रहे होंगे। → They will be playing.

**Uses:**

- Action continuing at a future time

---

### C. Future Perfect Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + will have + V3

**Hindi Clue:**

- चुका होगा, लिया होगा

**Examples:**

- मैं काम पूरा कर चुका होऊँगा। → I will have completed the work.
- वह घर पहुँच चुका होगा। → He will have reached home.

**Uses:**

- Action completed before a future time

---

### D. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

**Formula:**

- Subject + will have been + V1+ing + since/for

**Hindi Clue:**

- से ... रहा होगा

**Examples:**

- मैं दो घंटे से पढ़ रहा होऊँगा। → I will have been studying for two hours.
- वह सुबह से काम कर रहा होगा। → He will have been working since morning.

**Uses:**

- Continuous action up to a future point

---
